Governance
==========

SpikeInterface is a community-developed and maintained project seeking to facilitate the analysis of in vivo electrophysiology data. We welcome all contributors who are interested in sharing ideas or code to the project. These contributions are reviewed by members of the `maintenance team`_. All contributors including the maintainers are bound by our `Code of Conduct`_.


Contributions
=============

All contributions are made by opening issues or by pull request. Please see our `development docs`_ to read more about expectations
around formating of PRs. Anyone may contribute to an issue or pull request with comments, though we all commentators are expected to maintain the
focus of the issue/PR as well as follow the `Code of Conduct`_.

Maintenance meetings occur biweekly. Although they are generally private among the Core Maintainers, motivated contributors are welcome to request access to the meetings
and contribute at the meetings.
